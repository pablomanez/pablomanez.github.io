The Crypt is an Amstrad videogame we made back in 2018 and is entirely developed using CPCtelera and Z80 assembly. This project was an assignment for Videogames I to be presented to the CPCRetroDev contest.

The main objective is to kill all the enemies and get to the bottom of the crypt to win the game.

You can actually download it from the main 2018 CPCRetroDev results page (19!) or clicking directly on this link.

The Crypt was my first approach to assembly programming and the main reason Noxious Liquid took birth 1 year later.
