The Hive is a third person survival horror. By now is the bigger project I have been working, being everything (graphics, sound, models, animations, game architecture, textures, ...) entirely made from completely zero just using C++.

It was made in 8 months along with my teammates from GammaGames (Dd, David, Danny and Javi) in 4th year of Multimedia Engineering where all the subjects are united to make an unique videogame project.
