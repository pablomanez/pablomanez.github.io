Meat Starto! is a platformer game made entirely in C++ using the SFML library and Box2D physics engine.

With randomly generated zones in every playthrough, enemies and power ups it's a very fun and competitive game with support up to 4 players

It's a game inspired by The End Is Nigh and Super Meat Boy and most of the graphics are not of our own (you can see the Meat Boy) because is an assignment for Videogame Fundamentals I made with my team in 2018, my first developed videogame.

We are working on a upgraded version of the game, so, until further news, the game is not playable for the public =).
